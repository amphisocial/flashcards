Headline discipline ("action titles"): every slide headline (the "front" field) must state the conclusion or insight, never just the topic. A reader skimming only the headlines should be able to follow the entire argument.
- Weak (topic label): "Revenue growth"
- Strong (action title): "Enterprise adoption drove 42% revenue growth despite market headwinds"
Apply this to every layout except "quote" and "closing" (closing may use a forward-looking imperative instead, e.g. "Double down on enterprise in Q4").
